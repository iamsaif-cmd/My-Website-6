Frequently asked questions

Do I get a certificate after finishing a course?

Yes — every course includes a downloadable certificate once you complete all modules and the final project review.

Can I access courses on mobile?

Yes, the entire course library works on mobile browsers, and lessons stay in sync across devices automatically.

Is there a refund policy?

You can request a full refund within 14 days of purchase if you've completed less than 20% of the course content.

Do instructors give personal feedback?

Every project-based course includes at least one round of direct instructor feedback inside the lesson thread.
